Empowering Transparency in Resource Distribution

TransparentTrack is an offline-first, anti-corruption resource distribution app designed to ensure accountability and eliminate fraud in resource allocation. By leveraging biometric authentication and blockchain technology, we ensure that food and funds reach even the most vulnerable, phone-less beneficiaries in rural areas.

Key Features

Secure Access

Advanced biometric authentication ensures that only authorized disbursers can access the app.

Real-Time Analytics

Track resource allocation and disbursement in real-time with detailed reports.

User Management

Efficiently manage roles and permissions for disbursers.

GPS Tracking

Every transaction is GPS-stamped to ensure resources are distributed in the correct location.

Receipt Generation

Generate QR-coded receipts for beneficiaries to verify their transactions.
