Empowering Transparency in Resource Distribution

TransparentTrack is an offline-first, anti-corruption resource distribution app designed to ensure accountability and eliminate fraud in resource allocation. By leveraging biometric authentication and blockchain technology, we ensure that food and funds reach even the most vulnerable, phone-less beneficiaries in rural areas.

Key Features

Secure Access

Advanced biometric authentication ensures that only authorized disbursers can access the app. Role-based permissions restrict access to sensitive operations, ensuring accountability at every level.

Real-Time Analytics

Track resource allocation and disbursement in real-time with detailed reports. Monitor discrepancies and ensure transparency across all transactions.

User Management

Efficiently manage roles and permissions for disbursers, ensuring that only authorized personnel can initiate and approve transactions.

About Us

TransparentTrack is a cutting-edge platform designed to revolutionize resource distribution in underserved regions. Our mission is to empower NGOs, governments, and donor organizations with the tools they need to ensure transparency, accountability, and efficiency in resource allocation.

Global Reach

Supporting NGOs and governments worldwide in their efforts to combat corruption and ensure equitable resource distribution.

Community Focus

Designed specifically for rural and underserved communities, ensuring that even the most vulnerable populations receive the aid they need.

Secure & Reliable

Built with blockchain and biometric security to prevent tampering and ensure the integrity of every transaction.

Our Work in Action

Resource Distribution

Efficient and transparent distribution of resources to beneficiaries, ensuring that aid reaches those who need it most.

Biometric Verification

Ensuring only registered beneficiaries receive resources through secure biometric authentication.

Data Synchronization

Real-time data upload to secure cloud servers, ensuring transparency and accountability in every transaction.
